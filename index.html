Services

Recycling / Pay As You Throw / Solid Waste Services

SERA has extensive experience balancing solid waste management and environmental needs with short and long term economic considerations. We examine a wide range of options – both traditional and innovative – to develop practical solid and hazardous waste management systems. SERA’s approach is unique in that we base our analyses on real world data and sophisticated statistical and measurement approaches to provide the most reliable recommendations for clients. SERA has consistently been an innovator in the filed, but our experience makes us among the most cost-effective consultants for the job. We have successfully performed projects for clients across North America and even internationally. We work closely with our clients, and by combining on-site visits with our specialized expertise, our clients find we can consistently outperform local consultants because we do not need to “come up to speed”. We bring both local and national perspectives to our projects.

Energy Consulting Services

SERA’s energy consulting practice is diversified, covering evaluation, measurement, economics and program analysis. SERA’s energy consulting emphasis for the last few years has been focused in several key areas including: non-energy benefits, measure retention, greenhouse gas reductions, program evaluation (including MT and education programs), and forecasting and modeling. Our work is designed to help programs increase their overall energy efficiency goals and cost effectiveness. Skumatz is an experienced economist with over 20 years of experience in energy utility economics. Her staff includes former utility employees, and our energy clients include utilities across the nation. Dr. Skumatz has a national reputation and has been appointed to several widely recognized task forces and advisory committees in energy. She has been in business since 1990, and is a certified WBE.

Non Energy Benefits and Evaluation Services

SERA is the leading firm nationally in the area of non-energy benefits (NEBs). Our literature review and database incorporates the findings from more than 300 studies in the area, and we have developed credible estimation techniques for more than 30 categories of NEBs for utilities across the nation. SERA developed methods of measuring hard to measure benefits, and used innovative methods to quantify important utility, societal, and participant benefits from programs. SERA ‘NEB-It” model is literally the “state-of-the-art” model in the field. The model tailors NEB estimates for any utility and allows us to model and quantify NEBs for a wide variety of residential, low income, and commercial programs.

About Our Company

Skumatz Economic Research Associates, Inc. (SERA) is a research and consulting firm specializing in economic, policy, and evaluation analysis for solid waste and energy clients. Established in 1990, SERA has a nationwide client base including utilities, municipalities, counties, authorities, and state and federal agencies. We specialize in helping clients develop effective programs and strategies – and we use our expertise in integrated planning, evaluation, and economics to improve program performance and cost-effectiveness. SERA is a boutique firm, providing cutting edge analysis on specialized topics for our clients.

Energy

Our work focuses in several key areas: non-energy benefits (NEBs), attribution, free-ridership, net-to-gross (NTG), measure retention, program evaluation, surveys and market research, forecasting and modeling, and integrated planning. SERA’s “NEB-It” model represents the “state of the art”, and SERA has been a leader in measure retention, program evaluation, and measuring “hard to measure” impacts – publishing widely in these areas.

Solid Waste

SERA is the premier firm nationally in the area of Pay as you throw / variable rates (PAYT / VR), and in program evaluation and measurement. SERA has an extensive body of work in recycling policy / strategy analysis, forecasting and measurement, survey research, rate analysis, and policy evaluation for clients nationally and internationally. SERA maintains the largest database of solid waste programs for communities across the nation, which we use for performance benchmarking and quantitative analysis for our clients and our published research.

Sustainability / Net Zero / Environmental

SERA has experience in developing and evaluating programs designed to support sustainability goals for communities. We have designed, assessed, and evaluated wide-ranging strategies in energy efficiency, zero waste, water conservation, and air emissions. SERA has measured the dollar and avoidance impacts, impacts on metric tons of carbon equivalents (MTCE), job creation and economic development effects, environmental effects, and developed pioneering and defensible measurement methods for “hard to measure” impacts and “green goals”.

Our Amazing Team

SERA’s staff have “hands-on” experience, having worked within solid waste and energy agencies for many years. We support our research with a firm basis in data and real world experience. We offer practical solutions based on our own follow-through experience, as well as our experience as consultants for numerous clients. We have established effective methods of working closely with clients across the nation, combining on-site time with our specialized national expertise. We bring a blend of national and local perspective to every assignment. Dr. Skumatz, SERA’s Principal, has more than 30 years of industry experience, she has taught scores of workshops, and regularly presents papers at professional conferences. She has been the keynote speaker at half a dozen conferences in the US and overseas and has received lifetime achievement awards from two national associations (National Recycling Coalition (NRC) and Solid Waste Association of North America (SWANA)) and the Colorado Association for Recycling. She is currently serving as the Mayor Pro-Tem of her home town in Colorado.
